#philosophy #happiness

From a young age, we learn to embrace happiness, aiming for a life of ongoing contentment. We learn that the happiness we experience both in the past and in the present can only truly exist if its continuity remains unbroken. Any interruption or cessation of this happiness may lead to its negation.
