## Low susceptibility to FOMO 
Fear of Missing something
If you get median returns in above median time period, you will get extraordinary returns.

## Know the game that you are playing
Short- or Longterm basis. 
Know that if you are a long term player, short term variations should not affect you. 

## Have a long term horizon
The biggest advantage is to be allowed to stay investing for the next 10-20 years.

## Understand the difference between patience and stubbornness
Stubborness is persist in the error or thinking that something that works on the past will still work now

Patience is giving your strateggy enought time to succed
