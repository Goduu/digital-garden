<cite>
Sorry I wrote a long letter, I didn't have time to write a short one.
</cite>
Mark Twain

It's easyer to make something complicated and long and add a bunch of stuff to it. It's much harder to make it simple. To strip something down to the basic elements that it needs to explain this something to someone else.
